About Us

Sri Shukla Yajurveda Yuvaka Sangha is a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the teachings of Sri Yajnavalkya Rushi, the great sage and principal exponent of the Shukla Yajurveda. Formed by a collective of like-minded youth and scholars, the Sangha is committed to spreading awareness of Vedic heritage, conducting educational programs, and supporting traditional Vedic learning.

Through events, workshops, and community outreach, the Sangha seeks to inspire the younger generation to explore the depth of Vedic wisdom, especially the life, teachings, and legacy of Sri Yajnavalkya Maharshi, whose contributions form the cornerstone of Shukla Yajurveda tradition.
